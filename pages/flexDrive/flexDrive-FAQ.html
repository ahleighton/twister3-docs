Created by Jakob Voigts, last modified on Oct 13, 2014

Frequently asked questions and solutions.

We're posting questions and solutions at 'issues' on github, please post questions there and we'll get automatic notifications and resolve the problem asap.
Make sure to check the open and closed issues, most usual issues that can be resolved easily will be categorized as closed.
